## A Note on Methodology

All case studies in this portfolio are based entirely on publicly available information — breach notices, regulatory filings, news reporting, government correspondence, and official framework documentation. Analysis is conducted through a GRC lens and presented as portfolio work demonstrating applied analytical judgment. No proprietary information, client data, or confidential findings are included. Where information is not publicly confirmed, inferences are clearly labelled as such. The purpose of this work is to demonstrate how a GRC analyst thinks — how regulatory requirements, framework controls, and governance structures interact with real incident facts — not to make definitive conclusions about legal liability or organizational intent.
